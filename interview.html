Chef Ifeoma Okoye, Charlotte, NC

Background

Chef Ifeoma Okoye is a Nigerian cook based in Charlotte, North Carolina. Originally from Enugu, she moved to the United States over a decade ago and has become well known in her community for preparing delicious, authentic Nigerian meals for cultural events, family gatherings, and local celebrations.

Her love for cooking began at a young age in Nigeria, where she learned traditional methods and recipes passed down through generations. Today, she continues to share the rich culinary heritage of Nigeria through her meals, bringing people together around food and culture.
